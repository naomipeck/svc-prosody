In such constructions, V1 is fully inflected as normally for person agreement and TAM. Whatever the aspect of V1, V2 is in the imperfective aspect, that is, it has final stress and carries the IMPF suffix â€“aa where expected (whenever another suffix or enclitic attaches outside it, mostly with first plural or second subjects or objects).
